About us

We want to provide information about football (soccer) in Ireland in an easy to access way. We aim to provide free information about football that is useful for all ages. Such as 1. How to learn skills and how to train. 2. Nutrition, and what footballers can eat to optimise their performace. And 3. Show examples of other famous football players from Ireland.

Why is Football Important?

Good for communities

Football clubs are important for communities and bring a town/city together. Cheering on your local team, having a cup of tea and a chat provide great socialisaiton and entertainment for all age groups.
